Why That Name?

So where the heck did the name Of the Town come from? Well, Adema is our founders last name and it is both a Dutch and an African last name and in Africa the name means Of the Town. We are all about creating beautiful, cool products that elevate your swag and make your home the coolest in the town. We create people are for people who live in the town or city and absolutely love it. Cool. Current. Products that are both practical and gorgeous.
